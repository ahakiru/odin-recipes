Kumpanadas with salsa sauce

Easy recipe for empanadas with cheese and an accompanying salsa sauce.

  • 500g flour
  • 100g warm butter
  • 1 cup of water
  • a dash of salt
  • 3 tomatoes (500g)
  • 2 onions
  • 2 small chilis
  • 1 lime
  • silantro

Kumpanadas

  1. Mix flour, warm butter and water, add salt
  2. Roll the doe to a thin layer and cut out about 10-15cm large circles. Add cheesse in the middle and fold over. The edges can be fixed with a fork.
  3. Fry the kumpanadas in a pan with some oil on both sides.

Salsa sauce

  1. Use a mixer to cut all ingredients and mix well.
  2. Add salt and pepper as well as lime juice as desired.
  3. Let the sauce rest in the fridge for some time.
